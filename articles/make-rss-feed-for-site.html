I tried looking up how to make one, but found no information. I had to figure it out myself and I fonally got it. I hope this doesn't make me look dumb.

How to Do It

A simple XML file is all an RSS feed is. You can see what goes into the file here.

This means two things:

  1. It only takes a minute to make it, and

  2. All changes must be done maually

Feel free to just use my RSS file. Just copy and paste the "template" and add your changes.

If you want to allow readers to read the article within their RSS reader, you can try to copy and paste the contents of the article (either in plaintext or the HTML file's contents) into the "description" tag.
